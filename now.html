Now

What I’m up to, right now

Updated March 2026. Inspired by nownownow.com.

Work

I’m looking for my next senior role. Full-time or contract, remote, ideally somewhere with real infrastructure problems and a team that takes on-call seriously. Fintech, developer tools, or infra-heavy SaaS are the sweet spot. I’m interviewing actively and happy to do a paid trial.

Current projects

  • Job Bot. A small Node + SQLite tool I built to track remote DevOps listings from a handful of job boards and draft tailored CVs with Gemini. Write-up. It was supposed to take a weekend. It took three.
  • A tiny Karpenter cost audit tool I keep meaning to polish up and put on GitHub. Reads your NodePool manifests and compares your steady-state node distribution against the cheapest family that would fit the same pod profile. Currently lives in one Python file on my laptop.
  • Re-reading Google’s SRE Workbook. The chapter on alerting is worth it on its own. I read it about once a year and each time I catch an alert I wrote that violates at least one rule in it.

Learning

  • eBPF and Cilium, slowly. I have a local kind cluster I poke at on weekends.
  • OpenTelemetry. We have it half-deployed and I want to finish the job properly.
  • Go. I can read it fine, I’m getting better at writing it.

Not doing

I’ve stopped collecting certifications for the moment. I have four OCI certs and they were useful for what they were, but another badge on LinkedIn doesn’t move me anymore. I’d rather ship a real project I can point to.

What I do, in plain words

I look after the machines and pipelines that run a fintech transaction platform. Day to day that means EKS clusters, Terraform modules, GitHub Actions, ArgoCD, a lot of IAM, and a Grafana tab that’s always open. When something breaks at 2am, I’m the one paging in. When something doesn’t break, it’s usually because I already paged in at 2am three weeks ago and quietly fixed the root cause.

I care about three things, in this order: reliability, cost, and the developer experience of the engineers shipping on top of the platform. I treat all three as measurable, and I grind the numbers down over time.
